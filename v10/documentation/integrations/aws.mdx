## Configuring AWS Cloudwatch

#### 7. In the CloudTrail panel, select “Create trail”.

<img
  height="200"
  className="block rounded-xl"
  src="/assets/images/integrations/aws/welcome-page.png"
  alt="AWS"
/>

#### 8. Fill in the "Trail name" field.

<img
  height="200"
  className="block rounded-xl"
  src="/assets/images/integrations/aws/trail-name.png"
  alt="AWS"
/>


#### 9. Mark "Select all S3 buckets in your account".

<img
  height="200"
  className="block rounded-xl"
  src="/assets/images/integrations/aws/s3-buckets.png"
  alt="AWS"
/>

#### 10. Fill in the "S3 bucket" field. The name of the bucket must be unique in S3. Then click on “Create”.

<img
  height="200"
  className="block rounded-xl"
  src="/assets/images/integrations/aws/bucket-name.png"
  alt="AWS"
/>

#### 11. Click on the name of the trail to edit.

<img
  height="200"

  alt="AWS"
/>
