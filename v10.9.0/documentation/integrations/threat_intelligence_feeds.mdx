# Threat Intelligence Feeds Integration Guide

## Introduction

This guide provides instructions on integrating external threat intelligence sources with UTMStack, a comprehensive open-source Unified Threat Management platform. UTMStack offers a range of security services, including SIEM, threat detection, incident response, compliance management, and advanced reporting. By integrating threat intelligence feeds, you can enhance your organization's cybersecurity posture by leveraging real-time data on emerging threats.

## Features of UTMStack

UTMStack is designed to provide a holistic approach to cybersecurity through the following features:

- **Log Management and Correlation**: Real-time log collection and correlation for enhanced threat detection.
- **Threat Detection and Response**: Advanced detection and response capabilities using rule-based and heuristic analysis engines.
- **Threat Intelligence**: Correlation of threat intelligence databases to identify and mitigate threats such as spam, malware, and botnets.
- **Compliance Management**: Built-in compliance reports and dashboards for standards like HIPAA, GLBA, SOC 2, and more.
- **Advanced Data Visualization and Reporting**: Customizable dashboards and reports for security monitoring and compliance.

## Integrating Threat Intelligence Feeds

### Step 1: Log in to UTMStack Federation Service

Access your UTMStack Federation Service instance using the credentials provided during the installation process.

### Step 2: Enable Integration

Navigate to the **Integrations** menu and click on **+ Enable Integration** to begin the process of integrating external threat intelligence feeds.

![Integrations](https://mintcdn.com/utmstack/lxb2uSs0wybYno8q/images/import%20federation%20server/Integrations.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=lxb2uSs0wybYno8q&q=85&s=9fe2a3b0bd3236f12f03097cb193195f)

### Step 3: Configure Threat Intelligence Feeds

Configure the integration settings to ensure that threat intelligence data is accurately collected and correlated with existing security data. This step involves setting up data sources and ensuring seamless communication between UTMStack and the threat intelligence providers.
