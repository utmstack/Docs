# Case Management Guide

Welcome to the Case Management Guide for UTMStack version 10.9.0. This guide is part of the Incident Management group and focuses on creating and managing security investigation cases. UTMStack is a comprehensive cybersecurity platform offering advanced threat detection, incident response, and compliance management.

## Introduction to UTMStack

UTMStack is an open-source Unified Threat Management platform that provides a wide range of security services. It integrates SIEM, threat detection, incident response, compliance management, and advanced reporting to enhance cybersecurity. Designed for hybrid environments, UTMStack supports both on-premises and cloud deployments.

## Key Features

### Security Monitoring and Analysis

- **Log Management (SIEM):** Real-time log collection and correlation to identify threats.
- **Threat Detection and Response:** Rule-based and heuristic analysis engines for detecting and responding to threats.
- **Forensic Analysis:** Explore logs for detailed forensic investigations.

### Compliance Management

UTMStack simplifies compliance management by offering built-in reports and dashboards for major standards such as HIPAA, GDPR, and GLBA. Users can monitor security alerts, account activity, and generate necessary reports for auditors.

- **Supported Standards:**
  - HIPAA
  - GDPR
  - GLBA
  - SOC 2
  - FISMA
  - CMMC
  - PCI-DSS

### Customizable Dashboards and Reports

UTMStack provides a powerful dashboard and report builder that allows users to create, modify, and delete dashboards and reports without coding. This flexibility supports various compliance needs and forensic analysis.

### Integration Capabilities

UTMStack supports integration with various platforms and systems, including:

- Azure and AWS
- Hypervisors (KVM, HyperV, VMWare)
- Windows and Linux servers
- PaaS and SaaS applications like Office365
- Proprietary devices like CISCO and Sophos

## Incident Management

### Incident History Tracking

The incident management module includes a schema for tracking incident history, specifying required fields and allowed action types for incident-related events. Key action types include:

- INCIDENT_CREATED
- INCIDENT_MERGED
- INCIDENT_COMPLETED
- INCIDENT_ALERT_ADD
- INCIDENT_STATUS_CHANGE

## Summary

UTMStack offers a comprehensive suite of cybersecurity tools designed to manage and investigate security incidents effectively. With its flexible dashboards, robust compliance management, and extensive integration capabilities, UTMStack provides a holistic approach to threat management and incident response.
