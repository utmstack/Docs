# Audit Logging

## Introduction

In today's regulatory landscape, maintaining compliance with various industry standards is crucial for organizations. UTMStack's Compliance Management module offers a comprehensive solution for configuring and managing audit logs, ensuring adherence to standards such as HIPAA, GDPR, GLBA, and more. This guide provides an overview of how to leverage UTMStack's features to streamline compliance management and audit logging.

## Compliance Management Overview

UTMStack's Compliance Management module is designed to help organizations achieve and maintain compliance across multiple industry-specific regulations. It provides a holistic view of your organization’s regulatory compliance status by accommodating a multitude of standards. The module includes features such as:

- **Account Management**
- **Device Activity**
- **Application Audit**
- **Alerts and Incidents**

Each compliance standard has a dedicated section, allowing users to monitor specific activities, generate reports, and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Supported Compliance Standards

UTMStack supports a wide range of compliance standards, each with tailored dashboards and reports to facilitate monitoring and compliance management:

1. **Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)**
   - Includes reports on accounts validation, security alerts, login activities, and more.

2. **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**
   - Focuses on logon activities, vulnerability reports, and potential data privacy compromises.

3. **Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)**
   - Covers successful logins, file access, and policy changes in Active Directory.

4. **System and Organization Controls 2 (SOC 2)**
   - Provides reports on security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

5. **Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA)**
   - Offers reports for monitoring compliance with FISMA’s crucial sections.

6. **Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC)**
   - Designed for businesses bidding on U.S. Government contracts, with reports for different CMMC Levels.

7. **Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS)**
   - Ensures credit card data processing activities remain within PCI-DSS standards.

## Customization and Flexibility

UTMStack offers flexible, no-code customizable dashboards and reports for security monitoring and compliance. Users can create, modify, and delete dashboards and reports without writing a single line of code, thanks to the platform's proprietary data visualization and analysis engine.

### Adding New Standards and Reports

The Compliance Management module allows organizations to flexibly add new compliance standards and attach detailed reports to each standard. This feature ensures that specific business needs are met, and all regulatory facets are thoroughly covered.
