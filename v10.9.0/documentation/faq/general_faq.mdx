## System Requirements

To ensure optimal installation and performance of UTMStack, adhere to the following system specifications:

- **Data Storage Overview**: UTMStack uses hot log storage for immediate analysis and cold storage for archived data.
- **Server Specifications**: Recommended hardware specifications vary based on data volume. For example, 50 data sources generating 100 GB per month require 8 cores, 16 GB RAM, and 150 GB disk space.

For detailed system requirements, visit the [System Requirements page](https://documentation.utmstack.com/v10/Installation/system_requirements).

## Compliance Management

UTMStack streamlines compliance management by providing built-in reports and dashboards for major standards like HIPAA, GDPR, and GLBA. This module helps organizations monitor security alerts, account activity, and generate necessary reports for auditors.

- **Supported Standards**: HIPAA, GDPR, GLBA, SOC 2, FISMA, CMMC, PCI-DSS

For more information, visit the [Compliance Management page](https://documentation.utmstack.com/v10/compliance/compliance_management).
