## Introduction to UTMStack

UTMStack is an open-source security platform designed for Ubuntu 22.04 LTS. It offers advanced threat detection, log management, and compliance features, integrating seamlessly with SIEM and XDR capabilities. UTMStack is known for its strong security measures, including encrypted communications, containerized microservices, and robust authentication mechanisms.

## Features and Capabilities

UTMStack provides a range of features for comprehensive cybersecurity management:

- **Log Management and Correlation**: Efficiently manage and correlate log data for enhanced threat detection.
- **Threat Detection and Response**: Real-time response capabilities to identify and mitigate threats.
- **Security Compliance**: Generate custom reports and dashboards for compliance with standards like HIPAA, GLBA, GPDR, and SOC.
