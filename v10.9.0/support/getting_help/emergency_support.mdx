# Emergency Support

## Introduction

This guide provides an overview of emergency support procedures and escalation for UTMStack version 10.9.0. It is designed to help users understand how to access support and assistance during critical situations.

## Accessing Support

For immediate support, users can visit the [UTMStack Support Page](https://support.utmstack.com). This page provides access to various support resources, including documentation, online demos, and contact options for further assistance.

## Support Resources

### Documentation

The UTMStack documentation offers comprehensive guides and resources for getting started, installing, and configuring UTMStack. It includes information on system requirements, setup instructions, and integration options. Users can access the documentation at the following link: [UTMStack Documentation](https://documentation.utmstack.com/v10/Getting%20started/introduction).

### Online Demo

UTMStack provides an online demo to help users familiarize themselves with the platform's features and capabilities. The demo is accessible at: [UTMStack Online Demo](https://utmstack.com/demo).

## Compliance Management

UTMStack streamlines compliance management by offering built-in reports and dashboards for major standards like HIPAA, GDPR, and GLBA. These tools enable users to monitor security alerts, account activity, and generate necessary reports for auditors. More information can be found in the [Compliance Management Documentation](https://documentation.utmstack.com/v10/compliance/compliance_management).

## Dashboards and Reporting

UTMStack offers customizable dashboards and reports for security monitoring, compliance, forensic analysis, and uptime management. Users can create, modify, and delete dashboards and reports without writing code, providing flexibility for unique organizational needs. For more details, visit the [Dashboards Documentation](https://documentation.utmstack.com/v10/dashboards/dashboard).

## Summary

This guide provides essential information on accessing emergency support and resources within UTMStack. Users are encouraged to utilize the support page, documentation, and online demo for assistance. Compliance management and customizable dashboards further enhance the platform's utility in maintaining security and compliance standards.

For additional questions, please contact support via email at [support@utmstack.com](mailto:support@utmstack.com) or visit the [UTMStack website](https://utmstack.com).
